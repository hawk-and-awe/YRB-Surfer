WHAT IS THIS?
THE GRAVEYARD is a tool for surfing YouTube's hidden underbelly â€” the millions of videos uploaded with default filenames, zero views, and no intention of ever being found. Think of it as channel surfing through the internet's junk drawer.

HOW DOES IT WORK?
The surfer uses the YouTube Recycle Bin methodology â€” searching for default camera filenames, auto-generated titles, and forgotten upload patterns to find videos nobody was ever meant to see.
